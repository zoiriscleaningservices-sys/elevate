Standard House Cleaning

Our signature maintenance service. Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom sanitization, kitchen wipe-down, and trash removal to keep your home consistently fresh.

  • Kitchen countertops & stovetop sanitizing
  • Full bathroom cleaning & sanitizing
  • Dusting all accessible furniture & items
  • Floors vacuumed and thoroughly mopped
View Details

Ideal for homes cleaned within the last 3-4 weeks. We cover all basic tidying and deep wipe-downs of major touchpoints. Perfect for recurring schedules!

Deep House Cleaning

A comprehensive, heavy-duty cleaning that tackles accumulated dust, grime, and hard-to-reach areas. Highly recommended for first-time visits.

  • Hand-washing cabinet fronts & baseboards
  • Scrubbing tub/shower tiles & grout lines
  • Cleaning vents, light switches, & door frames
  • Dusting high light fixtures & ceiling fans
View Details

We dedicate extra time and elbow grease to remove built-up soap scum, grease, and dust. Perfect for changing seasons or preparing for guests.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Designed to make your transition seamless. We deep-clean every corner of an empty home, inside drawers, cabinets, and appliances to prepare it for move-in.

  • Inside refrigerator, oven, & cabinets included
  • Vacuuming tracks & dusting blinds/shutters
  • Thorough baseboard scrubbing & wall spot-cleaning
  • Total sanitize of bathrooms & kitchen utilities
View Details

Leaves the home spotless for new tenants or to satisfy lease agreements for security deposit returns. Includes all standard deep clean checklist items plus interior appliance packages.

Vacation Rental & Airbnb Cleaning

Fast-paced, hotel-quality cleanings between guest stays. We focus on sanitation, visual perfection, restyling, restock checklists, and guest-readiness.

  • Washing & changing linens/towels
  • Toiletries and amenities restocking tracking
  • Staging furniture, pillows, and decorative items
  • Immediate damage reporting & photo validation
View Details

We work on tight timelines to match check-in schedules. We ensure a consistent 5-star clean experience for your short-term rental guests.

Post-Construction Cleaning

Removing fine drywall dust, sawdust, paint splatters, and construction residues from a newly built or renovated home, rendering it completely move-in ready.

  • HEPA-filter vacuuming of air ducts & vents
  • Wiping fine dust from walls, ceilings, & surfaces
  • Scraping paint & labels off new windows/glass
  • Double vacuum/mopping of hard floors & tiles
View Details

Renovations leave microscopic dust that settles for weeks. We utilize specialized vacuums and microfiber techniques to trap and eliminate 99% of dust particles.
